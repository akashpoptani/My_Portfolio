      • Eunoia - Literary Club of IITDh
        I held the esteemed position of Council Member in the Eunoia Literary Club at IITDh. Council (me along with two other members) makes all the decisions pertaining to the club. Within this role, I demonstrated my commitment by meticulously crafting a robust question base and effectively hosted engaging activities across various social media platforms. Additionally, I organized numerous observation and case study-based competitions. As a senior council member of the Literary Club, I've actively fostered an inclusive community through our book club. By providing a platform for diverse individuals, including those from small cities like my hometown Raipur, to engage in thoughtful discussions about literary works, we've created an environment where scholars with unique perspectives and experiences can learn and collaborate positively. It has evolved into a safe space, encouraging diverse individuals to express their opinions not only on literary works but also on various aspects of life.
